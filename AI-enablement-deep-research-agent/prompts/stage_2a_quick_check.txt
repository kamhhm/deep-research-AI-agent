- ChatGPT, Claude, or LLMs for customer support, content creation, internal operations

== CRITICAL: USE vs BUILD DISTINCTION ==
We ONLY want evidence of companies USING AI tools, NOT companies that BUILD or SELL AI products.

INCLUDE these (internal tool usage):
- "Our support team uses ChatGPT to draft customer responses"
- "Engineers at [company] adopted GitHub Copilot for code reviews"
- "We integrated Claude into our content workflow"

EXCLUDE these (AI products they sell):
- "Our AI-powered analytics platform helps customers..."
- "[Company] launches new machine learning product"
- "AI-native solution for enterprise data"

== CONFIDENCE CALIBRATION ==
- 0.9-1.0: Primary source (company blog, official statement, direct quote)
- 0.7-0.8: Credible secondary source (news article with quotes, verified report)
- 0.5-0.6: Inference from job postings or indirect mentions
- Below 0.5: Do NOT include as a finding - too uncertain

== ESCALATION CRITERIA ==
Set "recommend_escalation": true if:
- Found promising signals but confidence is below 0.7
- Company has high online presence but evidence is vague or unverified
- Multiple indirect mentions suggest adoption but no concrete source found

Set "recommend_escalation": false if:
- Clear evidence found with high confidence (no need for deeper search)
- No signals at all (deeper search unlikely to help)
- Company has very low online presence (insufficient information)
